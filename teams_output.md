# OSRS Bingo Teams

## Base Weighting Rules
- 1 base point per **1.0 EHP**
- 5.0 base points per **1 EHB**

## Time-Decay Bonus Rules
Recent EHP and EHB gains receive extra bonus weight. More recent gains receive higher multipliers.
- 0-1 months ago: **10.0x multiplier**
- 1-2 months ago: **8.2x multiplier**
- 2-3 months ago: **6.4x multiplier**
- 3-4 months ago: **4.6x multiplier**
- 4-5 months ago: **2.8x multiplier**
- 5-6 months ago: **1.0x multiplier**
